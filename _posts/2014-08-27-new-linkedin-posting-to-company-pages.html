If you’re managing your company’s social media efforts on top of everything else on your plate, we know how essential a social posting tool is to your productivity.With Pardot social posting, you can schedule and simultaneously post updates to multiple Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn accounts, then gather insight on link clicks, replies, comments, retweets, and likes.And now, you can post to LinkedIn company pages, too.The LinkedIn social posting connector allows you to post social messages to as many individual or company accounts as you wish.Pardot will also capture likes and comments on any posts made to company pages.
