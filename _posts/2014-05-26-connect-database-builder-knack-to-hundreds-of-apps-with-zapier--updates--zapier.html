Knack, a simple online database and web app builder, is now on Zapier, allowing you to easily send data between it and over 300 other apps.With Knack and Zapier, you’re able to automate the insertion of a new record into Knack when you add a row to a Google Docs spreadsheet or when a new transaction is made in Stripe.Or you can send data out of Knack, too, automating the addition of subscribers to MailChimp or clients in Freshbooks.Danny Schreiber lives in Omaha, runs marketing at Zapier, and likes to run, read up on process and productivity, and try his hand at cooking.Follow @dannyaway
